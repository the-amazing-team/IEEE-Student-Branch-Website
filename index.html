Everyting about CITNC

Cambridge Institute of Technology – North Campus, located in Bangalore, is a symbol of academic excellence and innovation. Our campus, nestled amidst serene surroundings off the International Airport road, provides an ideal environment for learning. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and a team of highly experienced faculty, we offer a transformative educational experience to our students.

We take pride in our diverse student body and our unique industry-academic approach, which fosters collaboration and innovation. Our guiding philosophy revolves around nurturing future-ready entrepreneurs who are dedicated to global sustainability through technology and research. This commitment has earned us recognition, including accolades such as the Most Promising Student Branch award by IEEE Bangalore Section and the Best Emerging Engineering College in India – South 2018.

Accredited by AICTE and affiliated with VTU, our institution offers courses in Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. These programs empower students to excel in the ever-evolving technological landscape.

With a robust placement program and over 150 placement partners, we ensure more than 90% placements for our eligible students. This underscores our reputation as a premier institution for holistic education and career development. At Cambridge Institute of Technology – North Campus, we are dedicated to shaping the future leaders of tomorrow.
